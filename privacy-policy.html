1. Scope and Controller

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS app “MingZhu Technician” (Bundle ID: com.taimingzhu.ymz), operated by Zhiwei Jiang. The app is intended for service technicians to sign in, receive and manage service orders, upload service-related materials, use location-based check-in, and complete order workflows.

2. Data We Process

Depending on the features you use, we may process: phone number and SMS verification status; account and technician profile information; order, appointment and service records; precise location with your permission; photos you choose to capture or upload; and device, network, security and diagnostic logs.

3. Purposes

We use this data to authenticate users, review technician qualifications, dispatch and fulfill orders, calculate service distance, confirm arrival or check-in, process customer support and disputes, secure accounts, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, and improve app reliability.

4. Permissions

Location is used for technician positioning, distance calculation and check-in. Camera and photo-library access are used only when you choose to capture, upload or save qualification or service images. You may deny or revoke permissions in iOS Settings, although the related feature may become unavailable.

5. Service Providers and Sharing

We may use Amap services for maps and location, SMS providers such as Alibaba Cloud for verification messages, cloud hosting/storage providers for operating the service, and Apple services for App Store/TestFlight distribution. We do not sell personal data or use it for third-party advertising tracking. Service providers receive only the data necessary for their tasks and are required to protect it appropriately.

6. Retention and Deletion

We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law, accounting, transaction retention, dispute resolution or regulatory obligations. After account deletion, data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period unless retention is legally required.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent or account closure through an available in-app entry or by contacting us. We may verify your identity before processing the request and normally respond within 15 business days.
